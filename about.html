About Desert Dates Safaris

Desert Dates Safaris was founded in 2020 by Babuu Temba, a passionate safari guide dedicated to sharing the wonders of Tanzania’s wildlife and landscapes. In 2023, the company was proudly handed over to his son, Abdulkarim Temba, who continues the legacy with fresh energy and innovative safari experiences.

Based in Arusha, Tanzania, our company specializes in both day trips and multi-day safaris across Tanzania's premier parks including Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire, and more. Our fleet consists of three well-maintained safari vehicles, operated by over 10 professional and experienced drivers and tour guides.

At Desert Dates Safaris, we are committed to offering personalized, authentic, and memorable safari adventures that connect you deeply with Tanzania’s natural beauty and culture. Whether you are an international traveler or a local explorer, we tailor every journey to meet your expectations and exceed them.

Our Vision

To expand Desert Dates Safaris further by adding more vehicles, launching new safari packages, and offering luxury options, becoming Tanzania’s top choice for safari adventures.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced local guides with deep knowledge of wildlife and culture
  • Personalized safari itineraries to suit your interests and budget
  • Reliable and comfortable vehicles ensuring safe and smooth journeys
  • Commitment to responsible tourism and conservation efforts
