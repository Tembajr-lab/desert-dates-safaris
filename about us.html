About Desert Dates Safaris

Passion. Expertise. Authentic African Adventures.

Our Story

Desert Dates Safaris was founded in 2020 by Babuu Temba, a passionate safari guide with decades of experience leading guests through Tanzania’s most breathtaking landscapes. With deep roots in the tourism industry and a commitment to sharing the beauty of Tanzania, Babuu built Desert Dates Safaris to deliver personalized and unforgettable safari experiences.

In 2023, the company was handed over to his son, Abdulkarim Temba, who continues the legacy with modern innovations, top-tier service, and a focus on both local culture and international standards.
